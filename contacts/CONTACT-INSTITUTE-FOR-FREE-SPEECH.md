## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Contact form**: Submit the form at https://www.ifs.org/contact/ describing how your First Amendment rights to speak, publish, assemble, or petition are being violated.
2. **Scope**: IFS focuses on political speech, campaign finance, and related First Amendment litigation.
3. **Note**: Submitting the form does not establish an attorney-client relationship. IFS has not agreed to review submissions within any specific timeframe; urgent matters may require separate counsel.

## Scope

IFS is most relevant when speech restrictions involve political advocacy, disclosure requirements, or campaign-related regulation. Its work is less focused on technology-specific civil liberties than EFF, ACLU, or Knight Institute, but may support broad First Amendment challenges.
