## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Legal Assistance Request**: Visit https://www.eff.org/pages/legal-assistance to submit a request for legal help.
2. **Scope**: EFF takes on cases involving civil liberties in the digital world, including:
   - Free speech and code-as-speech issues
   - Coders' rights
   - Copyright and fair use
   - Government surveillance challenges
   - DMCA anti-circumvention
   - Privacy and encryption
3. **Process**: EFF reviews requests and takes cases that align with their mission and where they can set precedent.
4. **Note**: EFF takes a limited number of cases and prioritizes those with broad impact potential.

## Scope

EFF is the primary organization for technology companies and developers facing First Amendment challenges, surveillance overreach, copyright abuse, and DMCA-related threats to code distribution. Their track record includes the landmark Bernstein v. DOJ ruling establishing code as protected speech.
