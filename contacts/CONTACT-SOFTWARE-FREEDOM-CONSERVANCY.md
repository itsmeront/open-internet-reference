# Software Freedom Conservancy - Contact Record

## Public Contact Information

- Website: https://sfconservancy.org/
- About: https://sfconservancy.org/about/
- Become a member project: https://sfconservancy.org/projects/apply/
- GPL compliance inquiries: https://sfconservancy.org/copyleft-compliance/
- General contact: info@sfconservancy.org

## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **For FOSS projects needing a fiscal home**: Apply at https://sfconservancy.org/projects/apply/ for fiscal sponsorship and organizational support.
2. **For GPL compliance issues**: Contact through the copyleft compliance page if you believe your GPL-licensed code is being used in violation.
3. **Scope**: Conservancy helps with:
   - Fiscal sponsorship for FOSS projects
   - Legal defense of copyleft licenses
   - GPL enforcement
   - Right to repair advocacy
   - Organizational infrastructure (trademarks, contracts, governance)
4. **Note**: Conservancy primarily serves non-profit FOSS projects rather than commercial companies, but their compliance work protects the entire ecosystem.

## Scope

Software Freedom Conservancy is directly relevant to open source developers whose code is being used in license violation, projects that need organizational infrastructure, and the broader ecosystem that depends on copyleft enforcement. Their work ensures that companies using GPL-licensed code comply with its terms, protecting the rights of the original developers.
