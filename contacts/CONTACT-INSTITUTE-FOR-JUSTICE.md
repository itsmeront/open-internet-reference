## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Request help**: Visit https://ij.org/action/request-help/ to submit a case for consideration.
2. **Scope**: IJ litigates in several areas relevant to software companies:
   - First Amendment free speech (including information flow and economic speech)
   - Government overreach and regulatory abuse
   - Economic liberty (right to earn a living without arbitrary government restrictions)
3. **Process**: IJ represents clients at no cost and focuses on cases that will secure precedent protecting rights broadly.
4. **Note**: IJ's libertarian public interest approach means they are particularly strong on cases involving government restrictions on speech or economic activity.

## Scope

IJ is relevant to software companies facing government restrictions on code distribution, speech, or business activity. Their commitment to the free flow of information and opposition to government censorship aligns with defending software publication rights. They represent clients for free, making them accessible to smaller companies and individual developers.
