# Software Freedom Law Center - Contact Record

## Public Contact Information

- Website: https://softwarefreedom.org/
- Team: https://softwarefreedom.org/about/team/
- General contact: info@softwarefreedom.org

## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Contact**: Reach out via info@softwarefreedom.org describing your free software legal issue.
2. **Scope**: SFLC provides pro bono legal services to not-for-profit FOSS developers, including:
   - Licensing advice and compliance
   - Organizational structure and incorporation
   - Trademark protection
   - Patent defense
   - Legal education for FOSS communities
3. **Eligibility**: Services are provided to non-profit developers of free/open source software. Commercial entities may need to seek counsel elsewhere.
4. **Note**: Founded by Eben Moglen, who was instrumental in drafting GPL v3 and enforcing GPL on behalf of FSF.

## Scope

SFLC is directly relevant to non-profit open source developers needing legal guidance on licensing, compliance, organizational structure, or defense against patent and IP threats. Their expertise in free software licensing is unparalleled given their founding director's role in creating GPL v3.
