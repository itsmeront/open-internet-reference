# American Civil Liberties Union - Contact Record

## Public Contact Information

- Website: https://www.aclu.org/
- Contact page: https://www.aclu.org/about/contact-us
- Legal intake: https://www.aclu.org/need-legal-help
- Public address: 125 Broad Street, 18th Floor, New York, NY 10004

## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Legal Help Request**: Visit https://www.aclu.org/need-legal-help to submit a request.
2. **Scope**: ACLU takes on cases involving constitutional rights, including:
   - First Amendment free speech and free press
   - Fourth Amendment privacy and surveillance
   - Due process violations
   - Technology and civil liberties
   - Government censorship
3. **State affiliates**: ACLU operates through state affiliates. Technology-related constitutional issues may be handled nationally through the Speech, Privacy, and Technology Project.
4. **Note**: ACLU receives thousands of requests; they prioritize cases with broad constitutional significance.

## Key Attorneys for Software Rights

## Scope

ACLU is relevant to software companies facing constitutional violations — particularly government surveillance, First Amendment censorship, Fourth Amendment privacy issues, and due process in technology regulation. Their Speech, Privacy, and Technology Project specifically focuses on technology-related civil liberties.
