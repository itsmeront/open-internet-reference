## How to Engage

Cooley is a **commercial law firm** (not a nonprofit or pro bono legal organization like EFF or SFLC). Companies should contact them for paid legal representation in technology law matters.

1. **Technology industry practice**: Visit https://www.cooley.com/services/industry/technology for information on the firm's technology capabilities.
2. **Cooley GO**: Visit https://www.cooley.com/cooleygo for open source startup document packages and legal resources available to early-stage companies.
3. **Scope of representation**: Represents 1,000+ public and private software companies and 7,000+ high-growth private companies. Technology transactions lawyers combine IP and commercial transaction practices.
4. **Engagement model**: Companies engage Cooley as outside counsel for specific matters or ongoing advisory relationships. Standard commercial billing applies. Cooley GO provides some open source resources for startups.
5. **When to engage**: Technology companies needing counsel on IP transactions, venture financings, IPOs, M&A, technology licensing, or open source compliance. Early-stage startups may benefit from Cooley GO resources.

## Scope

Cooley is the #1 law firm representing VC-backed companies going public for the last 20 years. Represents 1,000+ public and private software companies. Cooley GO provides open source startup document packages, making some resources accessible without formal engagement.
