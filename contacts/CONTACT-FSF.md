## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **For licensing questions**: Email licensing@fsf.org for guidance on GPL compliance, license selection, or understanding free software licensing.
2. **For GPL violations**: Report GPL violations to the FSF licensing team, who work to bring violators into compliance.
3. **Scope**: FSF primarily helps with:
   - Understanding and complying with GPL and other free software licenses
   - Selecting appropriate licenses for new projects
   - Reporting license violations
   - Educational resources on software freedom
4. **Note**: FSF's focus is on promoting software freedom through licensing, education, and advocacy. For direct legal representation, SFLC or EFF may be more appropriate.

## Scope

FSF is relevant to any software company or developer working with GPL-licensed code who needs licensing guidance. Their compliance team helps resolve license violations cooperatively. They are the authoritative source for interpreting GPL family licenses.
