## How to Engage for Legal Help

1. **Legal Hotline**: For media law or press freedom questions, use the Legal Hotline form at https://www.rcfp.org/hotline/ — do not use the general contact form for hotline requests.
2. **Scope**: RCFP provides pro bono legal representation, amicus support, and resources for journalists and newsrooms on First Amendment, freedom of information, court access, and newsgathering issues.
3. **Pro bono attorneys**: Attorneys seeking pro bono opportunities should email Pro Bono Director Flavie Fuentes at ffuentes@rcfp.org with area of expertise and bar admissions.
4. **Note**: Contacting RCFP does not establish an attorney-client relationship.

## Scope

RCFP is primarily for journalists and news organizations. Software companies may find RCFP relevant when investigative reporting, security research publication, or anti-SLAPP issues intersect with press freedom, or when seeking coalition support on First Amendment policy.
