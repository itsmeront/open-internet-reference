## How to Engage

Wilson Sonsini is a **commercial law firm** (not a nonprofit or pro bono legal organization like EFF or SFLC). Companies should contact them for paid legal representation in technology law matters.

1. **Software industry representation**: Visit https://www.wsgr.com/en/services/industries/software.html for information on the firm's software practice.
2. **Internet litigation**: Visit https://www.wsgr.com/en/services/practice-areas/litigation/internet-strategy-and-litigation.html for First Amendment, privacy, and internet disputes.
3. **Scope of representation**: Represents 350+ public and private software companies. Handles IP transactions, venture financings, M&A, First Amendment litigation, privacy disputes, and copyright law.
4. **Engagement model**: Companies engage Wilson Sonsini as outside counsel for specific matters or ongoing advisory relationships. Standard commercial billing applies.
5. **When to engage**: Technology companies needing counsel on software IP, First Amendment challenges, internet litigation, privacy law, or venture-backed company formation and governance.

## Scope

Wilson Sonsini represents more venture-backed software companies than any other US law firm. Over 1,000 attorneys. The Internet Strategy and Litigation practice has secured landmark decisions involving social media, online advertising, copyright law, the First Amendment, and privacy. Founded over 60 years ago.
