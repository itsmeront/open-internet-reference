# Fenwick & West - Contact Record

## Public Contact Information

## How to Engage

Fenwick & West is a **commercial law firm** (not a nonprofit or pro bono legal organization like EFF or SFLC). Companies should contact them for paid legal representation in technology law matters.

1. **Technology Transactions practice**: Visit https://www.fenwick.com/services/practices/technology-transactions for information on the practice group's capabilities in copyright, data privacy, open source, patent, and trademark.
2. **Scope of representation**: Represents 1,500+ VC-backed companies. Practice grounded in copyright, data privacy, open source, patent, and trademark. Created industry-standard clean room protocols.
3. **Engagement model**: Companies engage Fenwick as outside counsel for specific matters or ongoing advisory relationships. Standard commercial billing applies.
4. **When to engage**: Technology companies needing counsel on open source licensing and compliance, software IP, clean room development, patent prosecution, technology transactions, or data privacy.

## Scope

Fenwick & West founded in 1972 in Palo Alto, pioneering technology law in Silicon Valley. Created one of the first software license agreements, the first shrinkwrap license (1976), and the first software clean room protocols. Over 500 attorneys. Technology Transactions practice explicitly covers open source law.
