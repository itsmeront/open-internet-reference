## How to Engage

1. **General inquiries**: Email info@epic.org for questions about EPIC's work or potential collaboration.
2. **Press inquiries**: Email press@epic.org for media requests.
3. **Scope**: EPIC works on surveillance oversight, consumer privacy, AI accountability, platform governance, health privacy, and open government through litigation, amicus participation, FOIA requests, and policy advocacy.
4. **Note**: EPIC is a research and advocacy center, not a general legal intake clinic for individual clients.

## Scope

EPIC is relevant to software companies facing surveillance technology regulation, biometric or tracking disputes, AI governance questions, or government data collection challenges. EPIC's amicus practice may support cases with broad privacy implications.
