# Open Source Initiative - Contact Record

## Public Contact Information

- Website: https://opensource.org/
- About: https://opensource.org/about
- Membership: https://join.opensource.org/
- License review: https://opensource.org/licenses/review-process
- Contact: info@opensource.org

## How to Engage

1. **For license questions**: OSI maintains the list of approved open source licenses and can advise on whether a license meets the Open Source Definition.
2. **For membership**: Organizations can join as affiliates to participate in OSI governance and advocacy.
3. **Scope**: OSI helps with:
   - Understanding the Open Source Definition
   - License approval and review
   - Advocacy for open source in policy contexts
   - Connecting with the broader open source community
4. **Note**: OSI does not provide direct legal representation but offers educational resources and community connections.

## Scope

OSI is relevant to companies seeking to understand or use open source licensing, wanting their own license reviewed against the Open Source Definition, or needing to connect with the broader open source advocacy community for policy purposes.
