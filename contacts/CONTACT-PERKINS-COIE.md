## How to Engage

Perkins Coie is a **commercial law firm** (not a nonprofit or pro bono legal organization like EFF or SFLC). Companies should contact them for paid legal representation in technology law matters.

1. **Technology Transactions & Privacy**: Visit https://perkinscoie.com/services/technology-transactions-privacy-law for information on the practice group's capabilities.
2. **Scope of representation**: Advises emerging and established technology companies on IP, privacy, open source compliance, software licensing, M&A diligence, and data privacy.
3. **Engagement model**: Companies engage Perkins Coie as outside counsel for specific matters or ongoing advisory relationships. Standard commercial billing applies.
4. **When to engage**: Technology companies needing counsel on IP transactions, open source compliance, privacy regulations, technology M&A, or software licensing.
