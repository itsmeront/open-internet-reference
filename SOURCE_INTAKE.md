# Source Intake

This file tracks imported project materials and their intake status.

## Intake Principles

- Imported project files are not automatically verified sources.
- Internal planning documents may guide architecture and workflow.
- Draft manuals and contact lists may suggest research leads, but their claims require independent verification.
- Contact information should be checked against official public sources before use.
- Substantive knowledge pages should cite primary or high-confidence external sources, not internal drafts alone.
