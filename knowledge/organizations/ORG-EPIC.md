# Electronic Privacy Information Center

## Summary

EPIC is a U.S. 501(c)(3) nonprofit research and advocacy center founded in 1994 to secure the fundamental right to privacy in the digital age through advocacy, research, and litigation.

## Verified Facts

- EPIC was established in 1994 to protect privacy, freedom of expression, and democratic values in the information age.
- EPIC describes its mission as securing the fundamental right to privacy in the digital age for all people through advocacy, research, and litigation.
- EPIC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with no clients, customers, or shareholders.
- Program areas include surveillance oversight, consumer privacy advocacy, AI and human rights, platform accountability and governance, telephone subscriber privacy, health privacy, and open government.
- EPIC routinely files amicus briefs, pursues open government cases, and speaks before Congress and judicial organizations.
- The official contact page lists address 1519 New Hampshire Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20036; phone (202) 483-1140; general inquiries info@epic.org; press inquiries press@epic.org.

EPIC's work on surveillance, consumer privacy, AI accountability, and platform governance is relevant to software companies facing government data demands, biometric or tracking technologies, and privacy-regulation compliance questions.
