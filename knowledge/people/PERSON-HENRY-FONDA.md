# Henry Fonda

## Summary

Henry Fonda (1905–1982) was an American actor. Contemporary reporting on the 2025 relaunch of the Committee for the First Amendment identifies him as among the original Hollywood members of the Committee during the McCarthy era. This OIR record documents that historical affiliation as reported in secondary sources; it is not a full biographical profile.

## Verified Facts

- CNN reported on October 1, 2025 that Hollywood legends including Henry Fonda launched the Committee for the First Amendment to stand up to McCarthyism.[^1]
- CNN reported that the Committee for the First Amendment was first created in 1947 during the McCarthy era.[^1]
- Jane Fonda's relaunch letter, as reported by CNN, stated she was relaunching the same Committee her father, Henry Fonda, joined with other artists during the McCarthy era.[^1]

[^1]: `SRC-CFA-RELAUNCH-CNN` — Jane Fonda Relaunches Committee for the First Amendment (CNN). Secondary reporting; archival corroboration pending.

## Historical Context

Henry Fonda died in 1982. OIR includes this record because the 2025 Committee relaunch explicitly invokes his participation in the original coalition. Detailed history of the 1947 Committee should be sourced from archival materials, not news coverage alone.
