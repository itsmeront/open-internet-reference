# Support OIR

Open Internet Reference is a free, evidence-based knowledge base. Traffic and hosting costs grow as more people use the site.

**If you find this site useful, you can buy me a coffee.** Tips are optional one-time support through Buy Me a Coffee and help cover hosting, domain, and maintenance time.

## What tips are (and are not)

!!! warning "Not tax-deductible"
    Tips sent through Buy Me a Coffee are **personal support** for the maintainer and this reference project. They are **not** charitable donations to a registered nonprofit, and they are **not** tax-deductible in the United States or elsewhere unless your tax adviser tells you otherwise.

- Tips are **voluntary** and do not unlock special access or editorial influence.
- OIR remains free to read; tips do not buy placement in the knowledge base.
- For **tax-deductible** support of digital-rights work, use the [Organizations](../knowledge/organizations.md) listed in OIR (for example EFF, ACLU, or Knight First Amendment Institute), not this tip jar.
